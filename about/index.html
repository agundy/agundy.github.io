Growing up I was a natural explorer and inventor, playing with technology, tearing old appliances apart, reading everything I could get my hands on and creating new products was normal entertainment for me. Many 12 year olds dream of being sports stars, firefighters, rock stars, etc, I only wanted to be an engineer. Through high school I took many classes to realize this dream and then I went to Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute where I realized my dreams.

I graduated with a degree in Computer Science in December 2016. And I now work as a software engineer in Boston.
